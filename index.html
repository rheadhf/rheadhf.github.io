internet memes have been described as contemporary dadaism. here, i will explore the origins and implications of several memes.


YOUNG MAN

on october 21st, 2014, twitter user charmingyungman99 posted this photograph of himself posed outside of his princeton, new jersey home before the homecoming dance along with the caption "i am so excited to slow dance to iris by the goo goo dolls".


GUY FIERI

this is a photograph of american restaurateur and television personality, guy fieri describing the best cheeseburger he has ever eaten. it was taken on february 10th, 2013 when he appeared on the late show with david letterman. "when eating cheeseburgers," said fieri, "i want to be like the burmese python, my two mandibles attached only by an elastic ligament that allows them to spread apart"


APPLE CIDER VINEGAR

on june 11th, 2015, pinterest user ilovemysondaniel pinned the image of this bottle of apple cider vinegar to her personal page with a description detailing the practical uses of acv. some uses include consumption for weight loss, natural toner, and flea repellant for pets. on june 13th, 2015, pinterest user auntsusan58 commented on the post: "don't drink that, it's bone hurting juice."
